Grey's Anatomy

The medical drama series follows a group of young doctors who began their careers as interns at Seattle Grace Mercy West Hospital. Meredith Grey, one of the young doctors and the show's namesake, is the daughter of a well-known surgeon. Grey finds it difficult to maintain ties with her coworkers, particularly the hospital's former chief of surgery, Richard Webber, due to a previous relationship between them - Webber and Meredith's mother had a personal relationship when Meredith was a child.
