A woman's infatuation with a man is detailed in "Enchanted," in which the woman corresponded with him via email for a long time. She was "awestruck" when she eventually met him in New York City, but she was terrified she would never see him again and that he was in love with another female.

Swift's earlier discography of the Speak Now and Fearless albums captures the "fairytale" motif nearly flawlessly, both lyrically and melodically. This song is the fairytale age personified, from the story-like narrative to the beautiful and magical words employed, to the delightfully amusing melody.
