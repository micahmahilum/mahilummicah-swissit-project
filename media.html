THE 100 CHARACTERS

Characters From The 100 That I'll Always Keep In Heart

  • Clarke Griffin and Lexa Kom Trikru

    They reunited in the third season, when Lexa had Clarke brought to Polis. As Lexa worked to bring peace to their people, Clarke forgave her and they became allies again and later lovers.

  • RAVEN REYES

    Raven is a mechanic working on the Ark. From the start of the series, she lands on Earth and is very useful for delinquents thanks to her mechanical skills, to create or repair equipment.

  • EMORI, ECHO & HARPER

    Echo, Harper, and Emori are my favorites too. They might only be fictional people but they touched my heart in the warmest wy possible. I love them.

  • CLARKE GRIFFIN & NIYLAH

    Clarke and Niylah is the relationship between Clarke Griffin and Niylah. They met off-screen and Clarke has been trading with Niylah after she left her people back at Arkadia. During the course of Season Three and Four, the pair sleep together twice but do not pursue a romantic relationship.
